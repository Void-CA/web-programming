Biography

My full name is Ari Alejandro Castillo Amador. I was born and grew in Chinandega, Nicaragua, surrounded by my family's warmth and the cultural wealth of my surroundings. From a young age, I discovered a fascination with technology, which eventually led me to choose Cybernetic Engineering as my academic path.

Career and Education

Universidad Tecnologica de la Salle (ULSA)

In 2021 I graduated from the "Hogar Paulo VI" School, where on the advice of a professor I entered the "Universidad Tecnologica de la Salle" on his recommendation. At the beginning, although I was interested in technology, for personal reasons I preferred to enter studying Industrial Engineering, but as time went by, meeting people, my time watching university projects and various classes related to it, I preferred to change to a career in Cybernetic Engineering, which I consider was a great decision on my part. I am very happy to have taken it. Currently I'm at the 3rd year and I'm much more comfortable with what I am currently studying.
