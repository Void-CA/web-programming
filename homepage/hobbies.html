My Hobbies

My hobbies are an important part of my daily life, those little moments that I dedicate to free myself from the stress of everyday life, where I can clear my mind and find entertainment.

Chess

At the age of 13 I learned to play chess, since I always watched a friend of mine play, I asked him to teach me, at first it was somewhat tedious to learn, there are several concepts that are tedious at first, but after some time practicing with him, playing became an activity of my daily life, I started going to tournaments held regionally, rough my school and later as captain of the university team. My last tournament was pretty good, taking third place nationally to the university. Although I am not currently very active due to my studies, it is something that I am passionate about and practice frequently.

Videogames

Video games have been something that has accompanied me all my life, since I can remember I used to play video games, here in Nicaragua it was very common to find places called "Nintenderias" which is basically a center for renting video game consoles, around the age of 7 I used to go with my brother to play with the PS2, which at that time was very famous, then when I was 8 years old I had my first console, a DSi, it is a console that I have a huge love for, I really spent hours on it and hours with games I loved, Mario, Crash Bandicoot and Pokemon are just a few of the games I played. And to this day I still play video games when I can, I consider it to be very entertaining and beneficial in some aspects.

Movies

I like to watch movies, whatever their genre, if it's good it's sure to catch my interest. Many times when I watch movies, what I like most is to see other people's analyzes of the events in the movies. Many times, the movies leave people open to their imagination about certain events. I like the part of theorizing about subsequent events. or prior to the contextualization of the film in question, and although the content that I consume the most is normally movies, sometimes I also tend to watch series or animes, lthough it is not so common. I usually prefer a movie for its length, having an entire story in about 2 hours.
